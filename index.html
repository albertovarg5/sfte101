Tamarindo

Tamarindo is a prime spot for surfing, sportfishing, diving, and sunning. The beaches host nesting leatherback turtles from October to May. You can find gorgeous beach houses, B&Bs, and luxury hotels here. Perfect for sampling the true flavors of Costa Rica.

Jaco

The tiny hamlet of Jaco on Costa Rica's Pacific Coast is a paradise for sun-seekers and active travelers, offering beautiful volcanic-sand beaches, lush rainforests, and verdant mountains. It's a great destination for surfers, families, and honeymooners.
